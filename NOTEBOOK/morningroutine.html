MORNINGS



DURING SCHOOL

During the school week, I have much less time to get ready. I often have less than 45 minutes to get ready if I want to get a full amount of sleep. However, also often I will sacrifice half an hour of sleep for the sake of relaxation in the mornings.

The following table is a general guideline of the ideal morning routine, and hopefully I will be successful in following it, for the most part. Leaving room for laziness here.

5:25-5:30 Wake up - don't have to get out of bed at this point, just waking up and getting yourself ready.
5:30-6:00 Shower and Skincare - On days where you shower, this is the time for that. You may do the first half of your skincare in the shower. The order of skincare (01.10.19) is as follows -
  1. (ed)Charcoal cleanser + (3d/w)spinbrush
  2. (ed)Burt's Bees gentle foaming cleanser
  3. (4d/w)Acure scrub + salicylic acid scrub
  4. (ed)Thatcher's Witch Hazel Rose Astringent
  5. (ed)Andalou sensitive Rose serum
  6. (ed)Lush Enzymion moisturizer
  7. (in summer)Krave sunscreen
6:05-6:30 Breakfast - Time to make a good breakfast! should be under 350 calories, unless you have orange juice. There is always room for orange juice. Some ideas for breakfast -
  • Chik'n + Egg + Avocado, Strawberries, OJ
  • Slice of bread with banana mash + smoothie
  • 1 cup overnight oats with fruits
  • Egg + English muffin + apple
6:30-6:40 Eat - Enjoy, but quickly!
6:40-6:50 Prepare - Pack bag (hopefully it was already), and then brush teeth. Relax if done early.
6:50/6:55 Leave - If at dad's house, leave at -50. if at mom's, you may leave at -55.




ON WEEKENDS

On the weekends, generally, I will have more time. in the mornings, and be able to get a decent amount of sleep. However, it is basically the same, with different times.

9:00 Wake up - Much more relaxing time allowed, its the weekend after all!
9:15 Exercise - Do something physical to wake your brain up, this can be yoga, simple stretches, dumbell weight lifting, etc.
9:30 Shower and Skincare - On days where you shower, this is the time for that. You may do the first half of your skincare in the shower. YOu may do a mask if you feel so inclined. The order of skincare (01.10.19) is as follows -

  2. (ed)Burt's Bees gentle foaming cleanser
  3. (4d/w)Acure scrub + salicylic acid scrub
  4. Mask, unless sheet, then do after next.
  5. (ed)Thatcher's Witch Hazel Rose Astringent
  6. (ed)Andalou sensitive Rose serum
  7. (ed)Lush Enzymion moisturizer
  8. (in summer)Krave sunscreen
10:15-10:45 Breakfast - Time to make a good breakfast! should be under 350 calories, unless you have orange juice. There is always room for orange juice. Some ideas for breakfast are in morning routine, but here are some more i may have more time for:
  • banana pancakes
  • Bagel sndwch w chik'n, egg, avo, tomatoes, etc
10:45-11:15 Eat & relax - Watch a video or a netflix episode, and relax while you eat. If you feel inclined to read, please do!
11:45 Plan Day - Sit down for a little and plan how you want your day to go; whether you plan to stay home or go on many adventures.
