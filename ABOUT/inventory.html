THE COMPUTER

My computer is the most used electronic that I own, as I code, do school work, write, game, and do basically anything you can think of on here.

I have a 15-inch Macbook Pro, 16GB RAM, 256GB disk space, with a 2.8 GHz Intel Core i7 Processor (if you want any more details, contact me).

I have many productive apps on here, with only a few games. I try to keep my desktop clean and consistant using the Mac's "stacking" feature, but you know, stuff happens. I change every app icon to be B&W themed, with Apple/App store downloads having a white circle background with black lines, and Apps from the internet having a square black background with white lines for easy identifying.

Some of the apps i have on here include, but are not limited to:

  • Log by Josh Avanier, a time-tracking logger
  • Bear, a minimalistic writing app
  • Spotify, for the focus music and podcasts
  • AutoDesk Fusion 360, for times when I feel inspired to CAD
  • Xcode, to build my app Possibilities, along with other projects

Over-all, I hope to make this my most productive space, but keep a seperate space for leisure as well.
